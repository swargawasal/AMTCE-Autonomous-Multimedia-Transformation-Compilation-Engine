Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

AMTCE ("we", "our", or "us") operates an automated content creation and publishing platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our services, including our TikTok integration.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize AMTCE to connect with TikTok or other platforms, we may collect:

  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens issued by TikTok's API
  • Basic account information (open_id, username) as returned by the TikTok API
  • Video metadata (titles, captions, hashtags) that you create or edit using our tools

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate and publish content to your connected social media accounts on your behalf
  • Manage access tokens and perform automatic token refresh as required by platform APIs
  • Provide you with upload status, logs, and analytics about your content

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. TikTok API Data Usage

AMTCE uses the TikTok Content Posting API to upload videos on your behalf. All data received from TikTok is used exclusively for the purpose of operating the publishing functionality you have authorized. We comply with TikTok's API Terms of Service and Developer Policies.

4. Data Storage

Access tokens and credentials are stored locally on your device in JSON configuration files within the AMTCE application directory. We do not upload credentials to any external server.

5. Data Retention

You can revoke AMTCE's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's app settings (Settings → Security → Authorized Apps). Deleting the local token store files will also remove all stored credentials.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data stored by AMTCE. As AMTCE is a locally-run tool, you have full control over all data stored on your device.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact the development team.
